What is SOPA?

SOPA stands for the "Stop Online Piracy Act". It's a bill introduced by Lamar Smith (R-TX) in the House of Representatives of the United States and is intended to help combat piracy. While most people believe in the value of protecting content owners' rights and content, many are concerned that SOPA will do more harm than good and are concerned that, through unintended consequences, SOPA will enable widespread censorship.

SOPAJS is a project to build a small, easy-to-use bit of Javascript that website owners can add to their webistes to display bits of information about SOPA.
